Fares and passes

You can use cash value on a Clipper card. Adult customers receive a discount for using Clipper instead of cash on all routes except short hops (Alameda/Oakland and Pier 41/Ferry Building). Passengers with a Youth, Senior or RTC Clipper card receive the same 50 percent discount they do when paying with cash. For a complete list of fares, visit SF Bay Ferry.

Minimum Balance

You must have a minimum balance of $1.50 on an adult card or 75 cents on a Youth, Senior or RTC Clipper card.

Transfers

If you transfer from the ferry to Muni or SolTrans, you do not need a paper transfer. Clipper keeps track of your rides and automatically grants you appropriate transfer discounts. If you transfer to AC Transit, you will need to request a paper transfer from the ferry operator.
