Fares and passes

You can use a monthly pass, a Muni Passport or cash value on a Clipper card. You must have a minimum balance of $1.50 for adult Clipper cards and $0.75 for Youth, Senior and RTC Clipper cards if you are paying for your ride with cash value. No minimum cash value balance is required if you are using a Muni pass.

Monthly Pass:

A monthly pass gives you unlimited rides on Muni.

You have two options for adult monthly passes:

  • The "A" Adult Fast Pass offers unlimited rides on all Muni vehicles and can also be used to ride BART within San Francisco. However, if you have a negative cash value balance on your card, you cannot enter the BART system even if you have a valid Fast Pass on your card.
  • The "M" Adult Monthly Pass is a Muni-only pass that is good for unlimited rides on all Muni vehicles and is not valid for use on BART within San Francisco.

Discount Monthly Pass:

You can load a discounted monthly pass on a Youth, Senior or RTC Clipper card. Visit Discounts for information on how to obtain a Youth, Senior or RTC Clipper card.

Muni Passports:

You can use Muni Passports for unlimited rides on Muni, including cable cars. Passports are valid for one, three or seven consecutive days. Passports cost the same regardless of the type of card you are using.

Limited-use tickets:

You also can purchase limited-use Clipper tickets from ticket machines at Muni Metro stations. Available for one-way and round trips, these tickets can only be used on Muni and are available in adult, youth and senior fares. If you're visiting the area or only ride Muni occasionally, we recommend purchasing a limited-use ticket instead of a Clipper card.

Transfers:

Clipper automatically calculates the 90-minute transfer period on Muni. You must tag your card each time you transfer so that the card reader can confirm the transfer period is still in effect. The card reader will display the remaining transfer time that you have left on your card.

Golden Gate Transit and Ferry customers paying with cash value receive a 50-cent discount off the Muni adult cash value fare when transferring to a Muni bus, light rail vehicle or streetcar (discount does not apply to customers who have a Muni monthly pass). Customers who pay their Muni fare with cash value and transfer to Golden Gate Transit and Ferry receive a 50-cent discount for adults and 25-cent discount for senior, disabled or youth customers (the discount on the Golden Gate Transit and Ferry fares does not apply to those paying with a monthly pass on Muni).

When you transfer from BART to Muni buses, light-rail vehicles and historic streetcars within an hour using your adult Clipper card, you will automatically receive a $0.50 discount. The discount only applies to cash value fares. You must tag your card to the card reader on Muni to be charged the discounted fare.

Daly City BART to Muni Transfer:

You are eligible for two free rides on the 14L, 28, 28L and 54 Muni routes when transferring from the Daly City BART Station and paying your fare on Clipper. The first trip must be taken within 23 hours after exiting BART and the second within 24 hours for the free fare to apply. The free fare is stored automatically when you tag your Clipper card as you exit the Daly City station. You must also tag your Clipper card upon boarding Muni for proof of payment.

For a complete list of fares and pass prices, please visit the Muni website.
