Commuter Check®

Clipper ® and Commuter Check®

If you have a registered Clipper card, you can begin loading cash or passes onto it using your Commuter Check benefits. If you do not yet have a Clipper card, please see your benefits administrator to find out how to receive a free Clipper card through the Commuter Check program. Clipper works with the following Commuter Check options:

  • Commuter Check vouchers
  • Commuter Check Direct®
  • Commuter Check Card Prepaid MasterCard®

Commuter Check paper vouchers
If you use Commuter Check paper vouchers, redeem them just as you do now to add value to your Clipper card. You can redeem vouchers at participating transit agency ticket offices or Clipper retail locations.

Commuter Check Direct
You can add value to your Clipper card online using your Commuter Check Direct account. Look for the Clipper option when you log in to your account. Your value will be available by the first of the benefit month. You must tag your card to a card reader in order to load the value.

Commuter Check Card Prepaid MasterCard
The Commuter Check Card Prepaid MasterCard can be used to add value to your Clipper card at transit agency ticket offices, Clipper Add Value Machines, online at Clippercard.com, or through the Clipper Customer Service Center at 877.878.8883 (TDD/TTY 711 or 800.735.2929). If you add value to your Clipper card at a ticket office or Add Value Machine, the value is available instantly and is ready to use. If you add value through the website or over the phone, it can take up to 3-5 days for the value to be available, and you must tag your card to a card reader in order to load the value.

Find transit agency ticket offices and Add Value Machines.

To learn more about the benefits options listed above, please visit www.commutercheck.com
