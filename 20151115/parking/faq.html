FAQ

What garages are currently involved?

Please visit the Participating Garages page for more information.

Can I get a Clipper card at one of the participating garages?

No, garages will not sell Clipper cards. To order a card and add parking value, visit clippercard.com or call 877.878.8883.

Can I use my cash value balance to pay for parking?

No, you can only pay for parking with parking value.

Can I use the same card for transit and parking?

Yes, if you already use Clipper for transit - including Youth, Senior and RTC Clipper cards - you can add parking value to your card and begin using your card for parking.

Do I get a discount if I pay for parking with Clipper?

No, you do not get a discount, but paying for parking with Clipper is faster and more convenient because you don't need to stand in line to pay. Take a time-stamped parking ticket when you enter the garage, and insert your ticket at the exit gate and tag your Clipper card to pay when you leave.

Can I use Clipper to buy my monthly parking pass?

No. You can only use parking value on Clipper for pay-as-you-go parking, not monthly passes.

What happens if my balance is too low to pay for my parking fee?

If your parking fees are greater than your parking value balance, you will not be able to use your Clipper card to pay. You will have to use another payment method like cash or a credit card. Signing up for Autoload will ensure you always have value on your card.

Does Autoload work for parking?

Yes, you can set up Autoload for parking, but it works differently from Autoload for transit. Learn more here.

What is the difference between "FasTrak", "SFpark" and "Park with Clipper"?

Park with Clipper is an SFMTA pilot program that allows Clipper cardholders to pay for parking with their cards. The program is being tested at five SFMTA-managed garages in San Francisco.

SFpark is a separate SFMTA pilot parking management program that aims to improve parking availability and reduce congestion by adjusting parking pricing and guiding drivers to available parking spaces. Some garages that are part of the Park with Clipper program are also part of SFpark. You can pay for parking at SFpark meters with your credit, debit or SFMTA parking meter card. These meters do not accept Clipper cards.

FasTrak allows drivers to automatically pay bridge and express lane tolls as well as parking at San Francisco International Airport.
